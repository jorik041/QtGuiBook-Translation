Mark Summerfield

Mark graduated with a degree in computer science in 1993 from the University of Wales Swansea. He followed this with a year's postgraduate research before going into industry. He spent many years working as a software engineer for a variety of firms before joining Trolltech. He spent almost three years as Trolltech's documentation manager, during which he founded Qt Quarterly and cowrote C++ GUI Programming with Qt 3. In 2005 he cowrote C++ GUI Programming with Qt 4, and in 2007 he wrote Rapid GUI Programming with Python and Qt. Mark owns Qtrac.eu and works as an independent trainer and consultant specializing in C++, Qt, Python, and PyQt.
